How Organizations run Mentorship Programs

To Bring Your Mentee And Mentor On One Platform And Start Mentorship Program, Get Your Organization Registered

Sign Up

Organization can register here by filling their details or register via social media. They need to upload documents for verification.

Once the documents are verified, organizations can purchase the subscription need for their mentee.

Organizations add mentee from here, mentee's will get mail with a link to complete the assessment form and create password.

Organization have full transparency over Mentee/Mentors profiles and can manage their profiles.

Organizations can create group sessions by selecting proper date and time Then invite mentee and mentors for the group session.

Organization will be able to view the listing and the details of all the sessions between mentee and mentors

Organization can manage their account, change password, view transaction history, view subscriptions.
