About

Gaye and David Wymond combined their optical and business expertise to begin Eyes Right Optical in 1992.

We wanted to bring to the Australian and New Zealand markets, frames of exceptional quality and operate a business that was efficient and streamlined.

Optical Wholesalers Australia & New Zealand

Since 1992, Eyes Right Optical has set the pace by distributing outstanding quality and award winning, design focused fashionable eyewear to the Australian and New Zealand independent wholesale optical markets. In a rapidly changing market place, service and quality have become the non-negotiable corner stones for success.

Customer service underpins and guides everything we do. Our latest independent customer service survey rated Eyes Right Optical #1 wholesaler for service and quality. We are very proud of our specially trained teams, both within our modern Distribution Centre and our energetic sales force, who follow through with precision every detail from first inquiry to final delivery.

Our “can do” philosophy is an integral part of the Eyes Right Optical culture. An industry leader in technology and innovative business solutions, we have invested heavily in providing “real time” information for our customers. Current stock on hand , on- line ordering, latest invoices and statements can all be easily accessed through our interactive website. Next day delivery of orders placed within the Practice is now possible via the internet with the implementation of our leading on-site “Sales Commando” software.

Quite simply, we understand the challenges of the independent optical market and understand people. There are no half measures at Eyes Right Optical!
