About us

Le Lumière Events with its unmistakably French origins, means ‘Light’.

Light represents our goal to brighten up people’s life with our events and premier lifestyle, infusing them with a little magic to escape from the monotony of day-to-day life. We shine through the life journey of our clients, lighting up the special occasions in every stages: birthdays, anniversaries, marriage, baby shower… We also serves as a spotlight to provide a stage for our guests to shine, be the star of the night.

Le Lumière Events offer premium event planning and guidance with management. Also to golf any company’s event with our luxurious touch. We are here to design the perfect party for you in all occasions. You name it and we will make it happen.

Meet our Amazing Team

Leanne Lam

Co- Founder & CEO

Leanne founded Le Lumière Events out of her passion for sharing beautiful things with people as well as creating enchanting, joyful moments for people with all things glamorous and fabulous.

A supporter of the belief that everything happens for a reason and that anything can be accomplished through supportive teamwork and collaborations, she strives to make the most of her and others’ potentials and to live life to its fullest.

Having lived in multiple places including Paris, London, Sydney, and Hong Kong, she recognizes the importance of slowing down in a fast-paced city and living in the moment. She finds peace by reading, discovering small cafes and celebrating all life can offer.

Louis Tong

Co- Founder & Managing Director

Being born and raised in Hong Kong, then educated in the UK enabled Louis to appreciate different cultures and also to acknowledge the importance of celebrating important moments in life. From a very early age, Louis was passionate about running events – Christmas and end of year proms were always exciting for him. He takes pride in helping everyone to enjoy remarkable occasions. He loves every moment of creating a whole event from scratch and seeing big smiles on guests’ faces. In his spare time, he enjoys going to the gym, cooking pasta and enjoy a nice glass of wine on a Sunday afternoon.

Louis’ core value is to bring more foreign and new ideas from the events industry into the local market, and also the concept of enjoying precious moments in peoples’ busy daily lives. Through LUX, he wishes to inspire the next generation within the creative and performing arts fields, and hopes to provide them a platform to shine and most importantly, allow the wider audience to appreciate their talents.
