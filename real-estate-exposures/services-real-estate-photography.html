An agent or broker has the training and skills to help people sell homes, but they do not have the skills of a professional real estate photographer. That’s why it’s smarter to hire a professional real estate photography service instead of struggling to use a point-and-shoot camera or smartphone to produce professional photography for a real estate listing.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF HIRING A PROFESSIONAL PHOTOGRAPHER?

A Powerful First Impression

These days, most property buyers start their search online. This gives the Realtor only a few seconds to make a powerful first impression in the mind of a prospective buyer. Low-quality photos taken with a smartphone will not have the wide angles, accurate color separation, alignment and professional editing provided by a residential real estate photographer. Hiring a professional photographer for real estate listings provides great benefit.

High-Quality Images

With the skills of professional real estate photography service providers like Real Estate Exposures, you will always have images that no amateur can produce. Front views are taken from the best vantage points, with excellent curb appeal and blue skies that entice buyers. Interior photos come out with perfect whites along with well-aligned vertical and horizontal lines. A professional photographer can help you get the high-resolution real estate photos your are envisioning for your listing.

Ability to Attract Qualified Buyers

More than 80 percent of home buyers now shop for homes in Central PA and Baltimore through services like Zillow. A reputable Central PA real estate photography service like Real Estate Exposures is essential to produce aerial videos, virtual reality tours and other high-tech services that attract buyers within and outside the country.

Attract More Home Sellers

After you invest in a professional real estate listing photographer, you can use the superior-quality images to showcase your outstanding services to future home sellers. Support your marketing efforts with professional photography for your real estate listing. Your one-time investment in top-quality real estate photos will continue to help you attract more leads and clients.

Faster Sales

Homes listed with professional real estate property photography will spend less time on the MLS list and sell at least three times faster than those with amateur images.

Potential for a Higher Sale Price

Homes photographed by a Baltimore real estate photographer with a DSLR camera will attract between $1,000 and $18,000 more than those photographed by agents using point-and-shoot cameras.
