ALL PREMIUM FLYERS INCLUDE

Full Bleed assures no unprinted edges in flyers. Your flyer will be printed from one edge to the other without any white borders that most personal printers must include.

Cardstock paper is thicker and more durable than regular paper used for printing. Although cardstock is thicker, it also is considered more flexible than regular printing paper as well.
